%----------RESEARCH INTERESTS----------
\section{Research Interests}
\vspace{3pt}
\small
My research focuses on \textbf{3D visual perception} for autonomous driving, particularly \textbf{semantic occupancy prediction} and \textbf{scene completion}. I am also interested in improving the \textbf{efficiency of multi-modal embodied systems} such as Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models, and exploring \textbf{test-time adaptation} as a practical approach to lifelong learning in open-world environments.
